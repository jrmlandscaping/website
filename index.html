What We Do

  • Lawn Care Services

    Including mowing, edging, blowing, raking, yard debris clean up, pruning, and tree trimming. Yearly or seasonal agreements available.

  • Beds & Planting

    We plant or transplant flowers and shrubbery and weed your flower beds. Yearly or seasonal agreements available.

  • Mulching

    Mulching and borders around flower beds, shrubbery, and trees.
     
     

  • Irrigation

    Expansion of existing system. Repair and replacement of sprinker heads, control box and electrical components, pumps, and pipes. Irrigation system winterization and Spring start up.

  • Drainage

    Add drainage to protect existing grass and plants. Poor drainage can kill grass and plants as well as attract mosquitos and other pests.

Meet the JRM in JRM Landscaping

René and Jessica Muñoz

Our goal is to provide service to our clients satisfaction guaranteed. We offer lawn care and landscaping services that include mowing, planting, pruning, lawn maintenance, edging, trimming, and drainage. We are also proud to offer irrigation system repair and irrigation system winterizing.

Although we are a new company, we have 15 years of landscaping experience. We started our company to provide lawn and landscaping services for our clients in the same way we would care for our own property. We are proud of our work and we want you to be too.
